Discussion
The phylogenetic analysis of all COI sequences available in GenBank revealed novel patterns, which alter current views on species delineation and phylogeographic patterns in spider mites. In addition, we found that a number of accessions are probably registered under a wrong species name. This may in the past have led to erroneous interpretations of phylogenetic patterns that included these GenBank accessions.
